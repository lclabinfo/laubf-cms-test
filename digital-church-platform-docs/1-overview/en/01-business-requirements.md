# 01. Business Requirements

## Executive Summary

This document defines the business goals, market analysis, competitive positioning, and revenue model for the Digital Church Platform.

---

## 1. Vision & Mission

### Vision
To become the leading digital platform for churches worldwide, enabling them to expand their reach and deepen engagement with their communities.

### Mission
Empower churches of all sizes with enterprise-grade digital tools at accessible pricing, enabling them to establish online presence, manage members, and grow their ministry.

### Core Values
- **Church-First**: Every feature designed with church workflows in mind
- **Accessibility**: Enterprise features at accessible pricing
- **Scalability**: From 50-member churches to mega-churches
- **Security**: Enterprise-grade security and compliance
- **Innovation**: Continuous advancement with AI and modern technology

---

## 2. Market Analysis

### Target Market

| Segment | Size | Characteristics | Priority |
|---------|------|-----------------|----------|
| **Small Churches** | < 200 members | Limited budget, basic needs | High |
| **Medium Churches** | 200-1,000 members | Growing needs, more features | High |
| **Large Churches** | 1,000-5,000 members | Full feature requirements | Medium |
| **Mega Churches** | > 5,000 members | Enterprise, white-label | Medium |

### Market Size (US)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Churches | ~380,000 |
| Churches using digital platforms | ~45% |
| Annual ChMS market size | $1.2B+ |
| Annual growth rate | 8-12% |

### Market Trends

1. **Post-pandemic digital acceleration**: Online giving and streaming now essential
2. **Mobile-first engagement**: 70%+ church interactions via mobile
3. **Integrated platforms**: Demand for all-in-one solutions increasing
4. **AI adoption**: Growing interest in AI-powered content creation
5. **Cost optimization**: Churches seeking flexible, pay-for-what-you-use pricing

## 8. Risk Analysis

### Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **Competitor Response** | High | Medium | Rapid feature development, price differentiation |
| **Technical Scalability** | Medium | High | Cloud architecture, load testing |
| **Churn Rate** | Medium | High | Customer success team, feature engagement |
| **Security Breach** | Low | Critical | SOC 2 compliance, security audits |
| **Economic Downturn** | Medium | Medium | Flexible pricing, freemium option |

### Regulatory Considerations

| Regulation | Applicability | Compliance Approach |
|------------|--------------|---------------------|
| **PCI DSS** | Payment processing | Stripe handles compliance |
| **GDPR** | EU churches | Data privacy controls, consent management |
| **CCPA** | California churches | Privacy policy, data deletion |
| **SOC 2** | Enterprise customers | Type II certification roadmap |
